EXPERIMENT

In this study, you are going to read a series of short passages describing passing conversations between either two acquaintances or two complete strangers.

You will be reading at your own pace one segment at the time. You will have to press the SPACE BAR to move on to the next segment. Every segment you read will disappear before the next one appears. Please make sure to MAXIMIZE your browser window.

The passages will be divided in two parts:

First, you will see a segment describing the scenario of the conversation.

For example, ‘Ron is waiting in line at an ATM when the woman behind him asks him:’

The second part of the passage is the actual comment that opens the conversation:

For example, ‘Excuse me, do you know if there is another ATM nearby?’

You will read the comment one word at the time by pressing the SPACE BAR at your own pace.

After you finish reading the comment, you will get a question to make sure that you have read the entire passage carefully. The questions may be about the scenario (the first part of the passage) or about the comment (the last part of the passage).

For example: ‘Did Ron know the person who was speaking?’

You will be given feedback on your response. You must respond correctly at least 75% of the time to complete the task.

Good luck!
